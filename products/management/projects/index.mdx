<sub>Updated March 12, 2026</sub>

Projects on Raff Cloud let you organize your resources — VMs, volumes, snapshots, and backups — into logical groups. Every resource belongs to a project, making it easy to manage access, track costs, and maintain separation between environments like production and staging.
