A **region** is a geographic location where Raff Cloud operates compute. The region you pick at VM creation determines where the VM's CPU, RAM, and disk physically live, and which IP ranges it gets.

## Available regions

| Region | Location | Status |
|---|---|---|
| `us-east` | United States (East) | Available |

<Note>
  Additional regions are planned. The [List Regions](/api-reference/catalog/list-regions) endpoint always returns the current set.
</Note>

## How region choice affects your VM

- **Latency** — pick the region closest to your users.
- **Pricing** — per-plan rates are region-dependent (see [Pricing](/products/build/virtual-machines/details/pricing)).
- **VPC scope** — a VPC and the VMs inside it must share a region.
- **IP allocation** — public IPv4 addresses are drawn from region-specific pools.

A VM cannot move between regions after creation. To migrate, snapshot the source VM, save the disk as a custom image, and create a new VM in the target region from that image.
